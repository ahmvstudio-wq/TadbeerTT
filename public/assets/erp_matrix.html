Three Golden Rules of ERP Implementation

  • Rule 1: Freeze Your Processes First. Do not write a single line of code or sign a software contract until your standard operating procedures (SOPs) are documented and optimized manually.
  • Rule 2: Prioritize User Adoption. The most technically perfect system is useless if your team rejects it. Involve department heads early in the sandbox phase to ensure workflows feel intuitive.
  • Rule 3: Establish a Master Data Plan. Migrating bad data into a new ERP is a recipe for system collapse. Clean, validate, and structure your client and inventory records prior to launch.
