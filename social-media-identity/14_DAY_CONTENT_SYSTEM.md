# 14-Day High-Impact Content System
## Tadbeer Transformations — Muscat, Oman

**Objective**: Build institutional credibility so that any decision-maker discovering Tadbeer within these 14 days concludes: *"These people clearly understand business transformation better than everyone else."*

**Background rules applied to every post**:
- White (#FFFFFF) or warm cream (#F9F8F3) backgrounds only. Never teal backgrounds.
- Deep teal (#184F5B) for primary typography. Gold (#CAA94C) for accent elements.
- Inter (English) / Cairo (Arabic) typefaces.

- **Muted (#555555)**: Secondary information. Dates. Locations. Metadata.
- **Never**: Bright colours. Gradients. Coloured backgrounds.

- Thin gold horizontal rules (1-2px weight, 30-50% frame width)
- Gold square bullet points (not round)
- Oversized gold numerals as background design elements
- Thin geometric border frames at 5-10% opacity
- **Never**: Icons from free icon packs. Stock photography. Decorative illustrations. Drop shadows. Rounded corners on content cards.

- Horizontal lockup (Oryx + wordmark) for closing slides and branded moments
- Oryx mark only (at reduced opacity) as a watermark on select posts
- Always on warm cream or white backgrounds
- Never distorted, recoloured, or placed on busy backgrounds
